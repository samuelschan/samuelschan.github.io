SAMUEL CHAN

I am a PhD student in Music and a MacCracken Fellow at NYU. Prior to New York, I have interacted with brilliant thinking partners in San Diego (MA 2018) and in Hong Kong (BA 2016).

My current projects focus on musical aversion, digital circulation, and politics in contemporary Hong Kong. I am intrigued by failures, complexities, and undesirabilities; I collect anecdotes, gossips, and h/rumors; I reflect on knowledge production, writing, and pedagogy in universities. I also sing. I co-administer Decolonizing Ethnomusicology and will co-edit the Student Union Blog for the Society for Ethnomusicology.
