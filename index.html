Samuel Chan

Hello!

I am a PhD student in Music/Integrative Studies at UC San Diego. In 2016, I graduated from the Chinese University of Hong Kong with a BA in Music (First Class Honors).

My writings focus on the interactions between sounds, politics, and technologies in Hong Kong in relation to critical university studies, digital humanities, and ethnic studies. Recently, I have been thinking about genealogies of undesirabilities, failures, and disjuntures, as well as the theorization potentials of anecdotes, gossips, and rumors. I like thinking in threes.
