Samuel Chan

Hello!

I am a PhD student in Music/Integrative Studies at UC San Diego. I graduated from the Chinese University of Hong Kong with a BA in Music (First Class Honors) in 2016.

My current projects focus on networks of sounds, politics, and technologies in Hong Kong in relation to critical university studies, digital humanities, and ethnic studies. Recently, I have been thinking about genealogies of undesirabilities, failures, and disjuntures, the theorizing potentials in anecdotes, gossips, and rumors, as well as the pragmatic politics of self-branding. I like thinking in threes.
