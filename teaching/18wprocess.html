Academic Integrity

All suspected violations of academic integrity will be reported. Examples include:

  1. Plagiarism. Using ideas of others as your own without citations—whether by copying, paraphrasing, or combining the work of several authors.
  2. Cheating. Submitting work done by others as your own; inventing data, examples, or bibliography; submitting similar work to multiple courses.
  3. Fraud. Misrepresenting emergencies to alter deadlines or requirements.
  4. Theft. Stealing papers, notes, files, or data collected or written by others.
